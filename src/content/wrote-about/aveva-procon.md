<p class="lead">I was the first designer hired at 8over8, brought on largely for my technical skill and experience debugging cross-browser issues rather than a portfolio of pretty screens.</p>

<img src="/uploads/procon-hero.png" alt="AVEVA ProCon contract risk management software" style="display:block;width:100%;border:1px solid var(--line2);border-radius:8px;margin:8px 0 32px">

<p>Between 2013 and 2017 I spent most of my time on <a href="https://www.aveva.com/en/products/contract-risk-management/">AVEVA Contract Risk Management Software</a> (formerly ProCon), the sole product of 8over8 before its acquisition by AVEVA. It audited communication between the company commissioning a build and the contractor hired to build it — aimed at mega-capital projects where costs ran into the tens of millions and overruns of 40%+ were common.</p>

<p>Over my time on ProCon, I moved it from a database-on-a-screen interface to something with a more considered approach to usability, working with each product owner to visualise features and get sign-off from product and development before any code was written.</p>
