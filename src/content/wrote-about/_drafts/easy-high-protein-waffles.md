A quick lovely dish found on the 'gram.

<!-- excerpt -->

| Serves | Cook Time |
| --- | --- |
| 3 | 15 min |

## Ingredients
| Ingredient | Amt |
| --- | --- |
| Egg | 2 large |
| Plain Whole Milk Greek Yogurt | &frac12; cup |
| Milk | 2 tbsp |
| Maple Syrup | &frac12; tbsp |
| Vanilla Extract | 1 tsp |
| Protein Powder | 1 scoop |
| Rolled Oats | &frac34; cup |
| Baking Powder | &frac12; tsp |
| Baking Soda | &frac12; tsp |
| Cinnamon | &frac12; tsp |
| Salt | &frac14; tsp |

## Steps
* Blend all ingredients in a blender until smooth
* Pour &frac12; cup of batter into preheated waffle iron, cook for 3-4 minutes
