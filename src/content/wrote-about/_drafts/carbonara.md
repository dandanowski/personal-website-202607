This dish is not terribly exciting, but very reliable to satisfy my boys.

<!-- excerpt -->

| Serves | Cook Time |
| --- | --- |
| 4 | 20 min |

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Amt | Prepped |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Dry Pasta | 500g | |
| Onion | 1 large | Diced |
| Garden Peas | 250g | |
| Bacon | 1 pack | Diced |
| Eggs | 4 large | Scrambled |
| Sour Cream | 1 small pot | |

## Steps
1. Put **dry pasta** on to boil
2. In a second, with heat on **medium high**, pot put **onions** and **bacon** on to cook the bacon through.
3. Add **garden peas** to the second pot and warm through.
4. Once **pasta** is cooked through, strain and add to second pot.
5. Add **eggs** to the second pot, turn heat down to **low** and stir through until eggs are cooked.
6. Add **sour cream** to second post and stir through.
7. Ready to serve
