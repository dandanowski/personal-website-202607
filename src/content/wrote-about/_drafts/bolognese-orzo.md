A quick lovely dish found on the 'gram.

<!-- excerpt -->

| Serves | Cook Time |
| --- | --- |
| 2-3 people | 30 min |

## Ingredients
| Ingredient | Amt |
| --- | --- |
| Beef Mince | 500g |
| Passata | 500g |
| Dry Orzo | 1 cup |
| Water | 1 cup |
| Beef Stock | 1 cube |
| Grated Cheddar | 1 handful |

## Steps
1. Brown the **beef mince** with a little oil
2. Add **passata**, **orzo**, **beef stock cube**, and **1 cup water**
3. Simmer for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally to prevent it from sticking
4. After 20 minutes, stir through with **grated cheddar**
