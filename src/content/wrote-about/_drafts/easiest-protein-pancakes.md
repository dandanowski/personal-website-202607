A quick lovely dish found on the 'gram.

<!-- excerpt -->

| Serves | Cook Time |
| --- | --- |
| 8 | 5 min |

## Ingredients
| Ingredient | Amt |
| --- | --- |
| Rolled Oats | 1 cup + 1 tbsp |
| Baking Powder | 2&frac14; tsp |
| Protein Powder | &frac14; cup |
| Eggs | 3 small |
| Greek Yogurt | &frac13; cup |
| Vanilla Extract | &frac14; tsp |
| Maple Syrup | 1 tbsp |
| Coconut Oil | 1 tbsp |

## Steps
* Blend **rolled oats** in blender until reaches a flour texture.
* Add in rest of ingredients and blend until smooth.
* Cook the damn pancakes
